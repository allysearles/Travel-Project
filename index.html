WHY MIAMI?

At the Beach, Life Is Different.

Visiting Miami is an unforgettable experience that combines relaxation with adventure. During the day, you can soak up the sun on Miami's world-renowned beaches, such as South Beach or Crandon Park Beach, where the turquoise waters and white sands create a picture-perfect setting. Beyond the beaches, explore the vibrant neighborhoods, each offering a unique cultural experience.

You can stroll through the colorful streets of Little Havana, where the aroma of Cuban coffee and lively music fills the air, or visit the Art Deco Historic District in South Beach to admire its iconic architecture. For nature enthusiasts, the nearby Everglades National Park beckons with its lush landscapes and wildlife encounters. Miami's daytime activities cater to every interest, ensuring a memorable and diverse experience for all visitors.
